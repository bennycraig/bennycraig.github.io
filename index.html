Hello there.


I am a senior at the University of Michigan studying computer science and data science. I am fortunate to work with Professor David Jurgens, Daniel Romero, Rada Mihalcea, and Joyce Chai as a research assistant.

I'm broadly interested in researching language technologies to build interactive multimodal language agents (e.g., chatbots and robots) that are socially-aware, able to assist humans across tasks and environments, including those deployed online or grounded to physical environments.

Particularly, I am interested in leveraging multimodal information to explore areas such as Interactive (dialog) Systems, Embodied AI, and Computational Social Science.

Previously, I was a natural language processing intern at Bytedance AI Lab and a software engineering intern at Tencent.
