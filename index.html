Hello there.


My name is Benny. I study computer science engineering at University of Michigan and moonlight as a musician. I am passionate about using tech to build a more equitable world.

I worked as a Full Stack Engineer and Team Lead at LearningClues, an education-tech startup whose search engine is used in over 450 courses at University of Michigan. As an engineer, I helped reduce server costs by migrating the backend to serverless REST APIs. I also aided in the redesign of their database to support secure LTI login. Additionally, I conducted user testing to guide the development of study features. As a team lead, I supported the team by developing project management documentation, organizing co-working sessions, and onboarding new engineers.

I served as a data volunteer at CAIR Coalition, a non-profit immigration law firm. I constructed a database to house their Immigration Judge Decisions, making hundrededs of documents easily searchable in support of their legal team.

As a transfer student passionate about mentorship and community-building in computer science, I have taken on various leadership positions. While at Grand Rapids Community College, I developed two years of experience tutoring calculus, physics, chemistry and writing classes. I formed the GRCC Programmers club to provide collaborative opportunities for students interested in developing their programming skills together.

I have an extensive background in research. As part of the MICUP Scholars Program, I worked as a research assistant at Michigan Technological University studying computer microarchitectures. At University of Michigan, I have worked as a research assistant in the Crowds + Machines Lab developing a JavaScript simulator to model collaboration in teams.
