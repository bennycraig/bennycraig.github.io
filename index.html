Hello there.


I am a graduating senior at the University of Michigan studying computer science engineering. I'm passionate about building technology to create a more equitable world.

I've worked as a Full Stack Engineer and Team Lead at LearningClues, an education-tech startup whose search engine serves thousands of students at University of Michigan. I volunteered at the non-profit law firm, CAIR Coalition. During my time there, I created a searchable database for their Immigration Judge Decisions. As part of the MICUP Scholars Program, I worked as a research assistant studying computer microarchitectures. As an assistant in the Crowds + Machines Research Lab, I contributed to simulation software to model team performance.

Outside of programming, I studied music for one year at the Oberlin Conservatory, and more recently I've performed with the Michigan Gamelan Ensemble.
